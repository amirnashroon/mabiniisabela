Tourist Spot





Malamawi White Beach

Unknown to many, the island province of
Basilan has some fine beaches. One of them is
the Malamawi Beach which is about 30 minutes
away from the capital Isabela City. The very
clean beach has fine white sands and turquoise
water excellent for swimming. The place is also
well secured. It takes about an hour by ferry
from Zamboanga City and a return trip within
same day is vary feasible.





Lampinigan Beach

located in the picturesque city of Isabela, offers
a serene coastal retreat along the Philippines'
shoreline. The beachfront stretches gracefully,
providing a peaceful haven for relaxation and
various beach activities. The sands feature a
soft golden hue that harmonizes beautifully
with the clear turquoise waters of the ocean.
